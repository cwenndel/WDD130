Overview

Purpose

The Purpose of this website is structure a consulting company with the goals to present the employers, the importance to implement tools that will help them in decisions make. Too many datas in companys are disorganized and good datas are importante to each company to make decisions. The website will supply consulting services and implement reports at real time, through this report the employers geting a good vision about theirs companies, and then, taking better decisions.

Audience

The target audience of this website is companies that aim to reduce decision-making time and visualize information clearly and accurately in real-time. Companies visiting the site will be able to view different types of dashboards that can be implemented. Our target audience will have a national scope, as our specialized consulting services will be provided remotely or in-person by appointment.

Personal-Site

Why will people come to your site?

Those who will come to my website are those who understand the importance of keeping their data organized, and the dashboards centralize all important information about the company's projects and initiatives, allowing for a clearer and broader view of the business's main needs and improvement points. With this, management has an extra aid to make decisions, as employees can act based on data. People will seek our customized and specific dashboard services for their needs, with a good interface and easy to use, competitive prices, and a wide variety of dashboard options. In addition, they will seek to find the ideal tool to meet the needs they are looking for to control and improve their indicators. What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? Visitors to my website expect to find ways to organize their company's data in a way that provides valuable information for decision-making. In addition, they will perceive that VisualDash's dashboards will provide them with models that display clear and accurate information, enabling better management and a wide variety of indicators that can be monitored in real-time.

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

Visitors to my website want to familiarize themselves and get the first contact with the services offered by VisualDash. They will identify a wide variety of options with easy-to-understand templates and may even customize their own dashboards by sharing with us what types of models they want to implement in the company. They will be able to view ready-made models and decide which models will be interesting for application.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

Depending on the first contact with the site, the customer may provide personal information such as contact phone number, email for a possible meeting and to discuss specific matters, or even explore the site further to get ideas for application in their company.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

Visitors to my site will come because we offer customized and specific dashboard services to meet their needs, with a user-friendly and easy-to-use interface, competitive pricing, and a wide variety of dashboard options. We will also highlight our differentials compared to other sites, such as the personalized service and exclusive resources we offer. Our diferencial in dashboards will be to provide efficiency in data analysis. We will treat the company's data in a way that the information visible on the panels will be reliable, allowing us to generate information effectively. By providing personalized services, we will give the customer personalized information that will be organized according to the business's needs. When working with data, we know the importance of having accurate and real-time information for decision-making. This transparency will be crucial because it will show managers what the vital points of the business are, allowing them to analyze the strengths and threats of their company. By identifying them, it is possible to give the necessary attention to make decisions.

Content

A tool that enables the visualization of metrics

Dashboard is a tool that presents the main information of a company, such as financial information, human resources, logistics, and others. It is a tool that enables the visualization of metrics and key indicators of the business. In addition to presenting a customizable interface, it provides managers with real-time information based on the company's stored data.

Dashboards centralize all important information about the company's projects and initiatives, allowing for a clearer and broader view of the business's main needs and improvement points. With this, management has an extra aid to make decisions, as employees can act based on data

interactivity

Dashboards offer a dynamic experience, where information can be filtered and manipulated to highlight changes over time, achieved results, and potential threats that can impact the company. With this interactivity, it's possible to access few or many data points based on a given time frame. It's also important to have an analytical view to understand the generated information and make decisions for improving the company.

Acess to data for decision making

A dashboard brings together multiple data sources in a single interface. This means that you can have a detailed overview of your business in real-time. By doing so, you reduce the time needed to compile reports, saving time and resources. Once the Dashboard is implemented, it will require monitoring so that the data presents real information on the overall performance of the company, but also of each department, in order to be able to interact between the areas of the company.
