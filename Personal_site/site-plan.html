Purpose

The Purpose of this website is structure a consulting company with the goals to present the employers, the importance to implement tools that will help them in decisions make. Audience

The target audience of this website is companies that aim to reduce decision-making time and visualize information clearly and accurately in real-time. Companies visiting the site will be able to view different types of dashboards that can be implemented. Our target audience will have a national scope, as our specialized consulting services will be provided remotely or in-person by appointment.

Content

A tool that enables the visualization of metrics

Dashboard is a tool that presents the main information of a company, such as financial information, human resources, logistics, and others. It is a tool that enables the visualization of metrics and key indicators of the business. In addition to presenting a customizable interface, it provides managers with real-time information based on the company's stored data.

Dashboards centralize all important information about the company's projects and initiatives, allowing for a clearer and broader view of the business's main needs and improvement points. With this, management has an extra aid to make decisions, as employees can act based on data

Images for the Home page

interactivity

Dashboards offer a dynamic experience, where information can be filtered and manipulated to highlight changes over time, achieved results, and potential threats that can impact the company. With this interactivity, it's possible to access few or many data points based on a given time frame. It's also important to have an analytical view to understand the generated information and make decisions for improving the company.

Images for the Page 2

Acess to data for decision making

A dashboard brings together multiple data sources in a single interface. This means that you can have a detailed overview of your business in real-time. By doing so, you reduce the time needed to compile reports, saving time and resources. Once the Dashboard is implemented, it will require monitoring so that the data presents real information on the overall performance of the company, but also of each department, in order to be able to interact between the areas of the company.
