Dashboard is crucial for monitoring and analyzing data.
❮ ❯

Dashboards empower companies to make data-driven decisions that improve performance by providing real-time insights and visualizations of key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics across various business functions. Moreover, dashboards can help companies monitor their progress towards strategic goals and objectives, allowing them to adjust their strategies as needed. By having a clear understanding of their data, companies can gain a competitive edge and drive growth.

Dashboards offer a dynamic experience, where information can be filtered and manipulated to highlight changes over time, achieved results, and potential threats that can impact the company. With this interactivity, it's possible to access few or many data points based on a given time frame. It's also important to have an analytical view to understand the generated information and make decisions for improving the company.
